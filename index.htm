3. Secure Payment System (Escrow)

Since Clarity AI operates on BNB, the escrow and payment system is fully on-chain, ensuring secure and transparent transactions between investors and KOLs. Instead of traditional BNB-based smart contracts, we use BNB programs to handle payments efficiently with low fees and high-speed execution.

👉 How it works:

  • Wallet Connection – Users connect their Metamask, or other BNB wallets to the Clarity AI platform.
  • On-Chain Data Fetching – The platform reads data directly from BNB blockchain using RPC calls.
  • Smart Contract Escrow – Payments are locked in an escrow token account until conditions are met.
  • Trustless Payouts – Funds are only released when the job is confirmed as completed. If not, buyers get a refund.


Once a buyer initiates a payment to a KOL, the transaction is recorded on BNB blockchain, ensuring full transparency. Funds remain in escrow until the buyer manually confirms the service has been delivered. If a dispute arises, Clarity AI’s AI-driven analysis & human review team ensures fair resolution.

🚀 Escrow Workflow on Clarity AI

1️⃣ User connects their wallet and selects a KOL.
2️⃣ Buyer deposits funds into the escrow contract, locking the amount until the job is done.
3️⃣ KOL completes the service, and the buyer marks it as “Completed”.
4️⃣ Clarity AI’s smart contract releases the payment to the KOL.
5️⃣ If the KOL fails to deliver, the buyer can trigger a refund.

Since BNB’s transaction fees are extremely low, the escrow process is cost-effective and instant, making it superior to BNB-based solutions.
